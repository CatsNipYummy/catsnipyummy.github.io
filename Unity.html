Ikelos


Ikelos is experimenting with Virtual Reality and Game Design with Sony PlayStation. Our goal is to create moments that can only be experienced in Virtual Reality through the rapid prototyping of games. We are focused on creating VR driven mechanics, while providing feedback that is juicy and meaningful, keeping the interactions simple, fun and novel. Through constant experimentation and playtesting we aim to push the boundaries of what can be enjoyed in VR. Working with mobile VR and controllers, we create delightful play. To read more about the project and all the prototypes, visit the project website http://www.etc.cmu.edu/projects/ikelos/

Platform : Unity3D and Google Cardboard

Contributions:

  • One among the three programmers who developed the prototypes
  • Came up with game mechanics unique to VR
  • Held the role of co-producer, resolving conflicts and meeting deadlines for each prototype
